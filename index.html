Tech Cycle

Mission Statement

To give new life to technology, repairing, refurbishing, and reselling

Details

Target Audience

The target audience is mostly teens who can't afford tech for the base price. But it would work for any age.

Involved Communities

The involved communities are people in the tech business with old resources they don't use anymore that they are willing to donate. Along with people who don't normally have tech to use.

What does TechCycle contribute to the community

TechCycle contributes technology for low prices to people in the community that can't afford it normally.
